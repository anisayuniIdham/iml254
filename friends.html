When people says, 'trio never works' well here we are proof that they are wrong! My trio consists of my three closest friends whom I've created lasting and meaningful relationships with. We met during our high school years when we were assigned to the same dorm rooms.

After SPM, we decided to work together at Pizza Hut for about 3 months! Over the years, we have gone through countless laughter, tears, and memories, but one thing has always remained constant - our bond. Despite our busy schedules and lives taking us different directions, we always find time to catch up and discuss our achievements and tribulations.

Our friendship is truly remarkable; it has stood the test of time, distance, and changing circumstances. We have always supported each other through the good times and the bad and have become a source of great comfort and motivation for one another. I am truly blessed to have such wonderful friends who have brought so much joy and happiness into my life. They are like family to me and have always been there when I needed them the most, and I couldn't be more grateful!
