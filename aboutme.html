Little things about me!

NUR ANIS AYUNI, is a name that captures the essence of a vivacious and graceful individual. My name, in Arabic, means 'light, candy, and elegance,' and it is a perfect reflection of my cheerful personality, exuding warmth and refinement in my actions. In my spare time, I adore immersing myself in the vibrant world of manhwa and manga, finding joy and excitement in the stories that I encounter. I can confidently say that I am a loving individual, especially towards those I hold dear. As an empathetic and understanding person, I take great pleasure in uplifting my loved ones, and I am always eager to provide a lending hand when needed. I am an individual who is full of life and passion, ready to tackle any challenge that comes my way with a positive attitude and a smile on my face, always shining like the morning sun.

I was born on the 29th of September 2004 at Hospital Kulai, Johor. Ever since I was born, purple and grey have been my favorite colors, and they make me feel at ease and comfortable. I have two plushies named Gyu and Lamby - they are my favorite companions, which I cherish dearly. I love to watch anime and read comics in my spare time, and nothing makes me happier than escaping into the world of my favorite anime characters. I also love to immerse myself in kpop, and my favorite boy band is TXT. Their music is so relaxing and mesmerizing, and it makes me feel calm and happy. Apart from that, I'm also a huge fan of LANY and their music - they have such amazing and memorable tunes that make me feel inspired and optimistic about life. I'm a person who is full of life and passion for music, art, and anime, and I'm ready to tackle any challenge that comes my way with a positive attitude and a smile on my face.

So, if you're looking for a cheerful and caring individual who loves to uplift those around them, I am here to make your day brighter. I am a friend who is ready to listen, advise, and provide emotional support when needed. If you ever feel like you need a friend, just remember, I'm always here and ready to lend a helping hand.
